What to bring

Come dressed in your running clothes and running shoes (or bring your running shoes to put on at practice). If you want, some people bring a water bottle, towel, and if it's a workout, spikes or flats.

What to expect

At the start of practice, people will be standing around the entrance to the IM fields talking. A few minutes after, a club officer will make announcements - what the run/workout is, dues, upcoming runs and races, etc. - and everyone will go out running.

We hope to see you out there!
